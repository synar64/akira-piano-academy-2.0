Scales — Module Guide

How It Works

Select a scale and press Start. The notes appear on the staff — play them in order on the piano. The active note highlights in blue.

Scale Encyclopedia

Click the SCALE display to open the Scale Encyclopedia. Browse Standard, Modal (Church) and Exotic (World) scales from around the globe.

Circle of Fifths

Use the Wheel icon to open the Circle of Fifths. Click sectors to jump to keys and see I-IV-V relationships.

Learning Tip

Start with Sprint mode and 1 octave. Once you can play a scale cleanly, switch to 2 octaves and Marathon to build muscle memory.
